About Us

Eau De Edouard is a luxury perfume house founded in 1996 in Paris by Edward Meyercord. Specializing in the creation of sophisticated and elegant fragrances, the company embodies the essence of Parisian elegance. With a revenue of $500 million for the fiscal year 2023, Eau De Edouard continues to thrive in the luxury market.

Our Products

  • Transparence: A sophisticated fragrance that embodies clarity and elegance with citrus, herbs, and warm woods.
  • Curiosite: A bold and adventurous cologne featuring citrus, exotic spices, and amber.
  • Arjay-45: A balanced perfume combining fresh pear, delicate florals, and smooth musk.
  • Candor: A complex scent of prosecco aromas, lush florals, and creamy vanilla.
  • Inclusif: Celebrating diversity with bright palm oil notes, forest aromas, and sandalwood.

Growth & Expansion

Eau De Edouard is committed to expanding into new markets in Asia and the Middle East. The company is exploring eco-friendly product lines and integrating AI into its marketing strategies to enhance customer satisfaction and drive growth.
